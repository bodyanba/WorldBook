Features

No Identity Verification

Instantly Accessible. No need for registration or approval from a central authority.

Safe

Worldbook.io never holds your funds. Security deposits and multisig trade wallets encourage safe, successful trades.

Decentralized

Trading takes place on a global P2P network of users running Worldbook.io on their own machines.

Private

No one except trading partners exchange personally identifying data. All personal data is stored locally.
